Athlete Ratings · All Measured Metrics

The Standard

Every score is a percentile against this roster — a 90 in Strength means the athlete out-lifts 90% of the team. An overall score and rank is only given to athletes who have completed all six tests — it isn't fair to score anyone on partial data.

Elite 80–100 overall Advanced 65–79 Solid 45–64 Developing under 45 ▼ category to develop next

How to read these ratings

Six categories. One standard.

Explosiveness (vertical + broad jump) · Upper Body (bench press) · Lower Body (front squat + deadlifts) · Rel. Strength (max pull-ups) · Speed & Agility (5-10-5) · Engine (mile). Balance measures how well-rounded an athlete is — 100 means every category is at the same level; the ▼ marks the category to develop next.

Fully Tested & Scored

Athletes who have completed the full six-test battery, ranked by overall.

Awaiting Full Testing

These athletes haven't completed the full six-test battery, so no overall score is given — that wouldn't be a fair comparison. Their tested categories are shown roster-relative, and a full score will populate as soon as the remaining tests are in.
